Fall Armyworm

Spodoptera frugiperda

Severity Level
High Risk

AI Agronomist Solution

Organic Control

Use Neem oil extract (Azadirachtin) spray every 7 days. Introduce natural predators like Trichogramma wasps or earwigs. Handpick visible caterpillars in early morning if infestation is localized.

Chemical Control (Use with Caution)

Apply Emamectin Benzoate 5% SG or Chlorantraniliprole 18.5% SC. Ensure rotation of chemicals to prevent resistance. Do not spray during windy conditions or near water bodies.

Preventive Measures

  • Deep summer ploughing to expose pupae.
  • Intercropping with Desmodium (push-pull strategy).
  • Regular monitoring using Pheromone traps (10/hectare).
